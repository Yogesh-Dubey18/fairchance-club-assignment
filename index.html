Core journey

Designed to feel emotional first, functional all the way through.

01

Subscribe with intent

Members choose monthly or annual access, select a charity during signup, and set the percentage of their fee they want routed to impact.

02

Log the last five rounds

Score entry stays simple: Stableford values from 1 to 45, each with a date, always retaining only the newest five results.

03

Enter the monthly draw

Every active subscriber is automatically included in the monthly reward engine, with random or weighted algorithmic draw modes.

04

Verify, reward, repeat

Winners upload proof, admins review submissions, and payout states move cleanly from pending to paid with transparent reporting.

Draw engine

A reward model that feels exciting without losing operational clarity.

The system supports 3-match, 4-match, and 5-match winners. Pool shares are enforced automatically, simulations can be run before publishing, and the jackpot rolls over if no 5-number match is found.

Winner verification

Operational steps are visible, audit-friendly, and easy for admins to manage.

Step 1

Winner notified

System alerts the member by email and dashboard status update.

Step 2

Proof uploaded

Member submits a screenshot of the original score source for review.

Step 3

Admin approves

Verification is approved or rejected, with a complete audit trail.

Step 4

Payout completed

Prize state moves from pending to paid and reporting updates automatically.
